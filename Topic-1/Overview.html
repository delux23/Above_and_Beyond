What do you mean by the team IOT?


  • IoT, or the Internet of Things, refers to the overall system of interconnected devices as well as the technology that enables communication between those devices and the cloud as well as within those devices.


    • Overview


  • Through the creation of new business models and innovation opportunities, IoT has the potential to completely transform sectors including healthcare, transportation, and manufacturing.
  • It is now simpler and more affordable to connect objects to the internet thanks to the mass distribution of affordable sensors, CPUs, and connectivity technologies like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.
  • This has caused a rise in the number of smart devices that can be monitored and managed remotely, including household appliances, wearable technology, and industrial gear.
  • For a number of reasons, IoT has emerged as a new trend. First, it has become simpler and more affordable to connect objects to the internet thanks to the widespread availability of affordable sensors, CPUs, and networking technologies like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.
